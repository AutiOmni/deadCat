Dead Cat - Here for the bounce

What Is Dead Cat?

Whether you're new to the stock market or a seasoned trader, timing the perfect moment to buy or sell can be a difficult and stressful task. Dead Cat carries the burden of identifying technical indicators, analyzing charts, and much more to help you time that bounce! Dead Cat is a Google Chrome extension that, by default, tracks the market's biggest drops of the day and alerts you when all signs point to a possible bounce in the stock price. Or track stocks of your choice with a series of technical indicators suited for your trading style. Nothing is guaranteed in today's volatile stock market, but having Dead Cat on your side can give you a paw up.

Technical Indicators Within Reach

Diving head first into the stock market can be overwhelming and dangerous for your bank account. With Dead Cat, you can observe how technical indicators look in real time. Study and follow along until you feel like you have mastered each one or just continue let Dead Cat do the heavy lifting while you enjoy the day.

What Do I Need?

Customizable Alerts

If Dead Cat's default settings are not what you're looking for, choose which technical indicators to be alerted on.

For Example:

  • Bollinger Bands
  • Moving Average (MA)
  • On-Balance Volume (OBV)
  • Relative Strength Index (RSI)
  • Moving Average Convergence Divergence (MACD)
